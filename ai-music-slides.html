Conclusion

We all face challenges whether learning, creative, professional, social or something in-between.

Paradox

Should you use AI or not?

Due to being lazy, busy, or uncertain, we often look for end-to-end wholesale solutions from technology including with AI.
But is this the best and only way to leverage AI? (and what are the costs?)
AI should be framed and utilized as a "variable use" tool.
AI should be viewed as a collabrator.
...because ultimately AI can and should aim at enabling and empowering us as human beings and human creators.
